Service Scope & Limitations

To ensure clarity and manage expectations, the following are outside our service scope:

  • Self-service campaign builders: We build campaigns and workflows FOR you (Done-For-You model). Self-service tools are on the roadmap for enterprise tier.
  • Infrastructure management: We provide hosted AI infrastructure. Your internal systems, servers, and databases remain your responsibility.
  • Third-party API maintenance: If external vendor APIs change or break, we will notify you but cannot control or fix issues originating from third-party services.
  • System redesigns: We integrate with your existing systems. Redesigning databases, CRM structures, or core business systems is not included.
  • Unlimited custom requests: DFY workflows are included within reasonable scope. Enterprise-scale or continuous custom development requires separate scoping.

These boundaries ensure we deliver exceptional work within our expertise while maintaining sustainable, high-quality service for all clients.
